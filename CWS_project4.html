Tourist places in Nagpur

Ambazari Lake

Ambazari Lake is a beautiful lake located in the heart of Nagpur. It offers a great spot for picnics, boating, and bird watching. The lake is surrounded by lush green gardens and is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.

Futala lake

Futala lake is a beautiful lake located in the heart of Nagpur.It has a great musical fountani show complete with light that dances with rythm of melody and songs. It is one of the prime location in Nagpur.

Nagpur Zoo

Nagpur Zoo is a popular tourist attraction in Nagpur. It is home to a wide variety of animals and birds, including tigers, lions, elephants, and many different species of birds. The zoo also has a beautiful botanical garden and is a great place to spend a day with the family.

Kasturchand park

The ground generally known as KP was part of the land ceded to East India Company by the treaty of 1818. It was under the control of British army (Madras Army) for many years. It had build few building on this ground as arsenal and had stored several tons of explosive gun powder till late 19th century.

Deekshabhoomi

Deekshabhoomi is a sacred Buddhist pilgrimage site located in Nagpur. It is the largest hollow stupa in the world and is considered to be one of the most important Buddhist pilgrimage sites in India. The stupa is surrounded by beautiful gardens and is a peaceful and serene place to visit.
